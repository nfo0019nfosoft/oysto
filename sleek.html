Sleek Series

OYSTO Sleek Series modular aluminum single glaze partition system offers a seamless look and lays more emphasis on the glass. It is recyclable and lightweight. It consists of a section dimension of 25mm x 25mm and is a clip-on system for easy glass installation.

  • Profile: 25mm x 25mm
  • Silver / Black / other anodised finishes available
  • Glass thickness supported: 10 / 12mm / 11.5 laminated glass
  • Single Glazing
  • Acoustical Insulation: 32 dB
  • Partition height upto 3000mm
  • Curve profile available
