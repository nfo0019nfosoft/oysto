Style Door

OYSTO Style door frame is the new alternative for the aging solid wooden door with similar sound insulation abilities at a relatively better price point. It can be used as a swing as well as a sliding door. It consists of a 42x75 mm / 40x75 mm / 30x65 mm profile to be used as a four sided frame with a layer of glass within the frame.
