Ultra Series - 75

OYSTO Ultra Series 75 modular aluminum single glaze partition system is designed to nullify any undulations that occur in gypsum partitions which are of 75mm width as an industry standard and tend to never be straight edged which steals from the aesthetics of the partition system. Due to its heavy profile the system offers brilliant rigidity. It consists of a section dimension of 25x80 mm and is a clip on system for easy glass installation.

PROFILE 25 x 80 MM
FINISHES Silver / Black / Other Anodised
DOOR FRAME Can be used with cover or rebate profile
GLASS THICKNESS 8, 10 and 12 MM
ACOUSTICS Single Glazing Acoustical Insulation: 32 dB
PARTITION HEIGHT Upto 3000 MM
